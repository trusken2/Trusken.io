About Trusken Supply Chains

Our Story

Trusken Supply Chains was founded with a mission to streamline the supply of building materials, petroleum, and diesel across the country. With decades of combined industry experience, our team recognized a gap in the supply chain for reliable, high-quality materials and energy products. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted partner for numerous construction firms, industrial businesses, and energy consumers.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide superior products and services in the building materials and energy sectors. We strive to build lasting partnerships with our customers by delivering unmatched value through our quality products, timely delivery, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading supplier of building materials and energy solutions across Africa, known for our integrity, quality, and innovation in the industry. We aim to revolutionize supply chains to make them more efficient, sustainable, and accessible to all our customers.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and responsibility in all our dealings.
  • Customer Commitment: We are dedicated to our customers' success by providing top-quality products and tailored solutions.
  • Quality Assurance: We ensure that all products supplied meet the highest industry standards, guaranteeing reliability and durability.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek innovative approaches to improve our services, ensuring the best supply chain solutions for our clients.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to promoting sustainable practices in our supply chain to reduce our environmental footprint.
